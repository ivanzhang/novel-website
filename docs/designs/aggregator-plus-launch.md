## Vision

### 10x Check
Transform from "better pirate site" to "Spotify for Chinese web novels" — ultra-low monthly fee (9.9 RMB/month), unlimited reading, personalized recommendations, community interaction, author revenue sharing. Readers no longer choose between "free but awful" and "expensive but legit."

### Platonic Ideal
A reading experience so good readers forget they're on a website — smart typography auto-adapting to screen, seamless night mode, zero-wait chapter preloading, AI-driven "you might like" recommendations, reader community (chapter discussions, reviews, reading lists), author dashboard (revenue, reader data, engagement). Tech: SSR for SEO, CDN acceleration, PWA offline reading, WebSocket real-time comments.
